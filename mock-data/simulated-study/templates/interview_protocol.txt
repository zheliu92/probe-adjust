POST-EXPERIENCE INTERVIEW PROTOCOL
Smart Home Controller UX Study
-------------------------------
Duration: ~30 minutes
Format: Semi-structured. Follow the participant's lead. Probe with "Can you tell me more?" as needed.

INTRODUCTION
------------
Thank you for completing both conditions. I'd like to ask you some questions about your experience. There are no right or wrong answers — I'm interested in your honest thoughts and feelings.

--- SECTION 1: Overall Impressions ---

Q1. Now that you've tried both interfaces, what's your overall impression of the experience?

Q2. Was there any moment during today's session that stood out to you — positive or negative? What happened?

Q3. How would you describe the experience to a friend who hasn't tried either system?

--- SECTION 2: Trust and Sense of Control ---

Q4. During the tasks, how much did you feel in control of what the system was doing?
    [Probe: Was there a difference between the two conditions?]

Q5. Were there any moments where you weren't sure what the system would do next?
    [Probe: How did that make you feel?]

Q6. How much did you trust the system to carry out your intentions accurately?
    [Probe: Did that trust change over time within a condition, or between conditions?]

--- SECTION 3: Comparing the Two Conditions ---

Q7. If you had to choose one interface to use at home every day, which would you pick and why?

Q8. What did you find most different between the Dashboard and Voice-First interfaces?

Q9. Were there tasks that felt much easier or harder in one condition compared to the other?
    [Probe: What made the difference?]

--- SECTION 4: Open Reflection ---

Q10. Is there anything about your experience today that we haven't discussed, and that you'd like to share?

CLOSING
-------
Thank you. Your feedback is very valuable. Do you have any questions for me?
